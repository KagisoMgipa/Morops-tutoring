Products and services

Services

Morops Tutoring Academy is committed to providing exemplary educational services to the youth within our community, fostering positive transformations in their lives. In pursuit of this mission, we proudly extend a diverse range of offerings, which include but are not limited to the following

seized learning

Quality one-on-one tutoring ranging between 30 mins - 2 hours

We uphold the highest standards of personalized instruction, ensuring optimal comprehension and engagement for our clients. Our tailored services encompass the convenience of flexible location arrangements, accommodating sessions either at your residence or our designated learning facility. During these sessions, our dedicated educators devote their undivided attention to each client, facilitating a comprehensive learning experience that fosters deep understanding and active participation.

Enhanced group learning

Better quality group tutoring sessions holding 8 - 15 students per class

Within this service, we convene at our designated facility alongside fellow students to collaboratively navigate strategies for simplifying and enriching the learning process. Through dynamic engagement and discourse, we collectively address challenges that may arise throughout the academic year, fostering a supportive environment for proactive problem-solving and skill enhancement.

Online learning

Online one-on-one learning

The learner seamlessly connects to our designated online platforms, including MS Teams, Google Meet,and Zoom, where they are promptly greeted by one of our dedicated tutors. Each session spans approximately two hours, with learners allotted an additional thirty minutes for pre-class preparation, ensuring optimal utilization of class time without compromising on instructional quality.

Embedded

Upgrading service

Upgrading students marks from BETHSAIDA

Morops Tutoring Academy has partnered with BETHSAIDA to provide specialized assistance to students who may not have achieved their desired marks and seek to enhance their career opportunities. Through this collaboration, we offer targeted support and personalized guidance to help students overcome academic challenges and fulfill their potential. Our tailored programs focus on skill development, exam preparation, and academic enrichment, empowering students to pursue their desired career paths with confidence and competence. By leveraging our expertise and resources, MorOps Tutoring Academy and BETHSAIDA aim to equip students with the tools they need to succeed academically and make informed decisions about their future endeavors. Whether it's bridging learning gaps, honing study techniques, or exploring alternative pathways, we are committed to helping students unlock new opportunities and realize their aspirations.

NBT and University application

Tertiary assistance and guidence

We provide comprehensive support for university admissions and National Benchmark Tests (NBT) preparations, ensuring students are equipped with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in their academic pursuits. Our tailored programs are designed to professionalize the application process, offering personalized guidance, exam strategies, and resources to maximize success. With a focus on academic excellence and individualized attention, we empower students to navigate the complexities of university admissions with confidence and competence. Whether it's mastering standardized tests, crafting compelling personal statements, or navigating the intricacies of the application process, MorOps Tutoring Academy is committed to helping students achieve their academic goals and unlock their full potential
